How to Play :-



* Start the game by clicking on "Roll Dice" Button.

* The number at the top of the dice becomes your current score and gets cumulatively added until "1" appears on the dice. That is when your current score becomes zero.

* Click on "Hold" button to make your current score, the actual score.

* First player to reach 100 is the winner of the game.
